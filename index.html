Uncle Joe and the Subtraction of Fractions that don't look like each other

A Three.js 3D game shell for practising Subtraction of Unlike Fractions.

Uncle Joe lands on a planet out in space. Shooting stars streak across the sky as he walks out from his rocket.

Controls: Use WASD / arrow keys on laptop, or the on-screen arrow pads on mobile.

Mission rule: Learn Mode and Practice Mode are for preparation. Uncle Joe gets the reactor reward only after you finish 10 Challenge Mode questions correctly in one sitting.

Ahead of him is a space chamber. When he approaches, the futuristic door slides open upwards. Inside, a computer challenges him to solve Subtraction of Unlike Fractions.

Learn Mode and Practice Mode help him prepare. When Challenge Mode is completed with 10/10 in one sitting, he receives the reactor reward, returns to his rocket, and flies to the next destination.
